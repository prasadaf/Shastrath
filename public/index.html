Shastrartha

Shastrartha is a type of intellectual debate prevalent in India. It was a kind of philosophical and religious debates in which scholars participated to reveal the inner meaning of Hindu scriptures known as the shastras.

Shastra Gyan

Your Guru

Chanakya was a skilled strategist and played a key role in the rise of Chandragupta Maurya, who went on to become the founder of the Mauryan Empire. He is also credited with laying the foundation for the first unified state in India. Chanakya's ideas and teachings have had a profound influence on Indian political thought and continue to be studied and admired to this day.
